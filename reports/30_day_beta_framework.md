## Beta Evaluation Metrics
The public beta will run for exactly 30 days before we reassess the strategic direction of FiduScan.

### Target Success Metrics
To consider the beta a definitive success and move toward full commercialization, we must hit the following targets by Day 30:

1. **Active Users:** > 500 Weekly Active Users (WAU).
2. **Scan Volume:** > 2,000 successful scans per day across all modalities.
3. **Uptime:** > 99.5% availability for the FastAPI inference endpoints.
4. **Accuracy Satisfaction:** < 2% of total scans disputed by users as false positives/negatives.
5. **Retention:** > 30% of users return to perform a scan in their second week.

### Evaluation Checkpoint
If metrics 1 and 2 are met, the platform has proven product-market fit.
If metrics 3 and 4 fail, core engineering and ML retraining must be prioritized before monetization.
