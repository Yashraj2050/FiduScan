## Core Metric Definitions
To evaluate the success of the platform and guide future engineering resources, we are tracking the following Key Performance Indicators (KPIs).

### 1. Acquisition & Activation
- **Signups:** Total number of users who successfully complete registration.
- **Activation Rate:** The percentage of registered users who successfully execute their first forensic scan within 24 hours of joining.

### 2. Retention
- **D7 / D30 Retention:** The percentage of users who return to perform a scan 7 days and 30 days after activation. 

### 3. Platform Usage
- **Scans per User:** The average number of media files processed by an active user per week.
- **Modality Breakdown:**
  - **Image Usage:** % of total scans. (Expected to be the highest volume).
  - **Audio Usage:** % of total scans.
  - **Video Usage:** % of total scans. (Expected to be lowest volume but highest compute cost).
