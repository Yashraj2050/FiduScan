## Beta Onboarding Strategy
To ensure high-quality feedback and a controlled testing environment, the beta launch will be managed through an invite-only cohort system.

### 1. Invite Workflow
- **Waitlist:** Users register interest at `fiduscan.com/beta`.
- **Cohort Selection:** We will select cohorts of 50-100 users per week, prioritizing journalists, researchers, and early-adopter tech communities.
- **Invitation:** Approved users receive an email with a unique, single-use JWT registration link.

### 2. Tester Onboarding
Upon first login, the user is presented with a welcome modal explaining:
- The experimental nature of the FiduScan platform.
- The meaning of "Confidence Scores" (i.e., a 90% score means high confidence, not absolute certainty).
- The limitations of current deepfake detection (e.g., highly compressed social media videos may yield false positives).

### 3. User Expectations
- **Data Privacy:** Users must explicitly consent to the retention of their uploaded media for the sole purpose of improving our internal models.
- **Service Level:** Uptime is best-effort. No Service Level Agreement (SLA) is provided during the beta phase.
