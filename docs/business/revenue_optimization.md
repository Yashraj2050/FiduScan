# Revenue Optimization Plan (v5.1)

## Overview
This document outlines the strategic roadmap to optimize Average Revenue Per User (ARPU) and Lifetime Value (LTV) across the FiduScan platform. 

## Revenue Targets
- **Target 1:** 50 Paying Users ($10k MRR) - Focus on Pro tier conversion optimization.
- **Target 2:** 100 Paying Users ($25k MRR) - Focus on Enterprise outbound sales scaling.
- **Target 3:** 500 Paying Users ($125k MRR) - Focus on self-serve B2B SaaS mechanics and API monetization.

## Growth Opportunities
1. **API Monetization:** Charging per API call for volume scanners (Newsrooms).
2. **Storage Monetization:** Charging for long-term retention of Evidence ZIP packages.
3. **Seat Expansion:** Transitioning single-user Pro accounts to multi-seat Team accounts.
