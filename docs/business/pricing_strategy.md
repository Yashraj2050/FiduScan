# Pricing Optimization Strategy

## Strategy Shift
Move from purely usage-based (Pay-as-you-go) to a Predictable Subscription + Overage model to guarantee MRR baselines.

## Tiers & Value Metrics
### 1. Free Tier
- **Value:** 10 Scans/month.
- **Goal:** Lead Generation.

### 2. Basic Tier ($49/mo)
- **Value:** 100 Scans/month. 1 Seat.
- **Goal:** Capture the long tail of individual freelance investigators.

### 3. Pro Tier ($199/mo)
- **Value:** 1,000 Scans/month. 3 Seats. PDF Reports.
- **Goal:** The default offering for small Newsrooms.

### 4. Enterprise Tier ($1,500+/mo)
- **Value:** Unlimited Scans. Unlimited Seats. Blockchain Anchoring. Advanced Cases.
- **Goal:** High margin corporate clients.

## Upgrade Strategy
- Introduce "Feature Gating": When a Pro user tries to click "Anchor to Blockchain," display an upgrade modal detailing Enterprise benefits.
- "Seat Limit Reached": Prompt upgrades to Enterprise when trying to add a 4th team member to a Pro account.
