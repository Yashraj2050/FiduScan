# Customer Profiles (ICPs)

## 1. The Fact-Checker (Media)
- **Role:** Investigative Journalist or Editor.
- **Pain Point:** Verifying the authenticity of viral images/videos submitted via social media during breaking news events.
- **Willingness to Pay:** Moderate ($100-$300/mo) paid via corporate card.
- **Feature Preferences:** Fast detection speeds, visual heatmaps, easy-to-read PDF exports to attach to internal editorial reviews.

## 2. The Forensic Analyst (Enterprise)
- **Role:** Digital Forensics Expert at a Private Firm or Law Enforcement.
- **Pain Point:** Maintaining a strict, legally defensible chain of custody for digital evidence.
- **Willingness to Pay:** High ($1,500+/mo) paid via annual invoice.
- **Feature Preferences:** Blockchain anchoring, JSON reports for API ingest into internal tools, immutable audit trails.

## 3. The Content IP Owner (Media/Enterprise)
- **Role:** IP Protection Manager at a Stock Photo or Music Label.
- **Pain Point:** Tracking unauthorized scraping of proprietary assets for Generative AI training.
- **Willingness to Pay:** High.
- **Feature Preferences:** High-volume Watermark embedding APIs and automated extraction sweeps.
