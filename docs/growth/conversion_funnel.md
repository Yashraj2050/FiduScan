# Conversion Funnels

## Upgrade Funnel: Free → Pro
**Target:** Individual creators, freelance journalists, small forensic agencies.
**Triggers:**
1. Hitting the 10 scans/month free quota limit.
2. Attempting to download a forensic report in PDF format (Pro feature).
3. Attempting to anchor evidence to the blockchain.
**Friction Removal:** Implement 1-click Stripe Checkout from the Quota Bar modal.

## Upgrade Funnel: Pro → Enterprise
**Target:** Large newsrooms, insurance agencies, cyber forensics firms.
**Triggers:**
1. Hitting the 500 scans/month Pro quota.
2. Attempting to invite a 4th team member (Pro allows up to 3).
3. Accessing Advanced Case Management features (Approvals, Multi-case exports).
**Friction Removal:** "Contact Sales" button triggering a Calendly booking for an immediate demo and custom SLA drafting.
