# Acquisition Strategy

## 1. Outbound Sales (Enterprise)
- **Target Audience:** Digital Forensics Agencies, Insurance Fraud Departments, and Law Enforcement.
- **Tactic:** LinkedIn Sales Navigator outreach providing a free customized sample Authenticity Report on a known manipulated public asset in their industry.
- **Conversion Goal:** 5 closed Enterprise deals in Month 1.

## 2. Inbound Content Marketing (Pro Tier)
- **Target Audience:** Journalists and Newsrooms.
- **Tactic:** A fully automated weekly newsletter/"Forensic Breakdown" blog post analyzing trending viral media. Each breakdown is generated using the FiduScan API and includes an embedded FiduScan Authenticity Score widget.
- **Conversion Goal:** Rank page 1 for "Deepfake detection tool for journalists".

## 3. Product-Led Growth (B2B2C API)
- **Target Audience:** Consumer platforms (e.g., Discord communities, specialized forums).
- **Tactic:** Offer a free Discord Bot/Slack Integration that allows users to type `/fiduscan [url]` to scan an image. The bot replies with a summary and a link to the full FiduScan platform to view the detailed heatmap (acting as a lead generation funnel).
