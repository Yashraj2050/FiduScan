# Revenue Strategy

## Pricing Restructuring
Based on beta feedback and willingness to pay, the pricing model is shifting from purely usage-based to a hybrid Seat + Usage model to capture enterprise value.

### 1. Free Tier (Lead Gen)
- 10 scans/month.
- No PDF exports. No blockchain anchoring.
- Goal: Acquire email addresses and validate organizational intent.

### 2. Pro Tier ($99/mo)
- 500 scans/month.
- Includes Authenticity Reports (PDF/JSON).
- Goal: Capture Newsrooms and small agencies. Self-serve checkout.

### 3. Enterprise Tier (Custom, starting at $1,500/mo)
- Unlimited scans (subject to fair use).
- Advanced Case Management, Multi-user RBAC, Blockchain Anchoring, SLA, Dedicated Support.
- Goal: Primary revenue driver. High-touch sales process.

## Expansion Revenue
- **Overage Billing:** Pro users exceeding 500 scans are automatically billed at $0.15 per additional scan to capture burst usage during news cycles.
- **Storage Fees:** Charging a premium for retaining Evidence Chain ZIP packages beyond 90 days.
