# Commercial Scaling Plan (v5.0)

## Overview
This document outlines the roadmap to scale FiduScan from 100 early adopters to 100,000 active users, focusing exclusively on acquisition, retention, and monetization engines without altering the core product architecture.

## Scaling Milestones

### 1,000 Users (Month 1-3)
- **Focus:** Organic content marketing and high-touch outbound sales for Enterprise.
- **Goal:** Reach $10k MRR. Optimize the Pro tier conversion funnel.

### 10,000 Users (Month 4-8)
- **Focus:** Viral loop optimization (Referral Program) and SEO dominance (Automated forensic blog posts).
- **Goal:** Reach $50k MRR. Launch programmatic ad spend targeting cybersecurity analysts.

### 100,000 Users (Month 9-18)
- **Focus:** API-first strategy. Integrating FiduScan into major social media platforms and news verification hubs as a B2B2C offering.
- **Goal:** Reach $500k+ MRR. Heavy emphasis on Enterprise API usage quotas.
