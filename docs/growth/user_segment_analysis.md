# User Segment Analysis

## Overview
Analysis of the 12 paid conversions from the initial 100-user market validation phase to identify our Ideal Customer Profile (ICP).

## 1. Digital Forensic Agencies (Highest Converting)
- **Profile:** Boutique firms hired by law enforcement or private corporations to verify digital evidence.
- **Conversion Rate:** 8% (8 out of the 12 paid users).
- **Behavior:** High utilization of the PDF Authenticity Reports and Evidence Chain exports. Low UI usage (prefer API).
- **Retention:** 95% 7-day retention.

## 2. Media Newsrooms (High Potential)
- **Profile:** Fact-checking desks at mid-to-large media organizations.
- **Conversion Rate:** 3% (3 out of 12).
- **Behavior:** Burst usage. Rapidly scan incoming user-generated content during breaking news cycles.
- **Retention:** 60% 7-day retention (Usage is tied to news cycles).

## 3. Individual Creators (Lowest Converting)
- **Profile:** Freelance photographers or videographers using FiduScan for watermarking.
- **Conversion Rate:** 1% (1 out of 12).
- **Behavior:** Very low scan volume. High churn risk.
- **Retention:** 15% 7-day retention.

## Strategic Pivot
Marketing and sales efforts will pivot entirely away from Individual Creators to focus 80% on Forensic Agencies (Enterprise Tier) and 20% on Newsrooms (Pro Tier).
