# Growth Dashboard Specifications

## Overview
The internal Growth Dashboard is designed for the Product and Marketing teams to track the viral coefficient, retention, and conversion metrics of the FiduScan platform.

## Core Metrics Tracked (10)
1. **DAU (Daily Active Users):** Unique users performing at least one action per day.
2. **WAU (Weekly Active Users):** Unique users per week.
3. **MAU (Monthly Active Users):** Unique users per month.
4. **Registration Rate:** Signups per day vs. unique landing page visitors.
5. **Activation Rate:** % of registered users who complete their first Investigation within 24 hours.
6. **Investigation Creation Rate:** Average investigations created per active user per week.
7. **Report Generation Rate:** % of investigations that result in a downloaded PDF/JSON.
8. **Export Rate:** % of cases resulting in a bulk ZIP export.
9. **Conversion Rate:** Free -> Pro / Pro -> Enterprise upgrades.
10. **Churn Rate:** Monthly cancellation rate of paid subscriptions.
