# Beta Execution Plan

## 1. Objective
Execute the FiduScan Closed Beta over a 14-day period to validate the v3.0 forensic platform under real-world conditions.

## 2. Beta Cohort Setup
The first 10 accounts are provisioned via magic links to enterprise cybersecurity analysts from our design partner program. 

## 3. Communication Plan
- **Day 1:** Welcome email with magic link and tester checklist.
- **Day 3:** Async check-in via Slack (`#fiduscan-beta`).
- **Day 7:** Mid-point sync and survey for UX friction points.
- **Day 14:** Final off-boarding survey and bug bash review.

## 4. Success Metrics Tracking
- Active Users (DAU/WAU)
- Investigations Created
- Evidence Records Linked
- Reports Generated
- Evidence Packages Exported
