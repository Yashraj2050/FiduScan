# Prioritized Implementation Backlog

## Overview
All identified gaps ranked by User Impact, Revenue Impact, and Technical Risk.

## Priority 1: High User/Revenue Impact, High Risk
1. **Enterprise Cases (Mock to Real):** 
   - **Why:** Case management is the core offering for the Enterprise tier ($1,500/mo). If the server restarts, all cases are lost. This must be fixed immediately.
2. **Evidence Chain (Mock to Real):** 
   - **Why:** Fundamental to the platform's forensic integrity guarantee.

## Priority 2: High Revenue Impact, Medium Risk
1. **Developer Portal (Missing to Real):**
   - **Why:** Required to unlock the API-driven volume scanning tier for Newsrooms and B2B clients.

## Priority 3: Medium Impact, Low Risk
1. **Audio & Video Watermarking UI/DB (Partial to Full):**
   - **Why:** While image watermarking works, the lack of persistence and UI for A/V formats blocks multimedia Enterprise deals.

## Priority 4: Low Impact, High Risk
1. **Blockchain Anchoring (Mock to Real):**
   - **Why:** While a strong marketing point, most users do not currently verify on-chain. Technical risk is high (RPC/gas fee handling), making it lowest priority for immediate conversion.
