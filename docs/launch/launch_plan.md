# Public Launch Plan (v4.0 GA)

## 1. Launch Timeline
- **T-Minus 7 Days:** Final code freeze, marketing assets finalized.
- **T-Minus 3 Days:** Soft launch to closed beta testers.
- **T-Zero:** Public availability (GA).
- **T-Plus 1 Day:** Press release and social push.
- **T-Plus 7 Days:** Post-launch retrospective.

## 2. Launch Channels
1. **Product Hunt:** Primary launch vehicle targeting early adopters and tech communities.
2. **LinkedIn:** Focused on enterprise security professionals and CISOs.
3. **Twitter/X:** Tech community engagement.
4. **Hacker News:** "Show HN" post detailing the forensic architecture.
5. **Press Release:** Syndicated to cybersecurity news outlets.

## 3. Launch Metrics
1. **Acquisition:** Target 5,000 unique visitors on launch day.
2. **Activation:** 500 new user signups.
3. **Engagement:** 1,000 initial media scans performed.
4. **Retention:** 20% D7 retention for organic cohorts.
