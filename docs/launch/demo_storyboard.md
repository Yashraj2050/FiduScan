# FiduScan Demo Storyboard

## Scene 1: The Problem (0:00 - 0:10)
- **Visual Setup:** Fast-cut montage. A fake news article headline, a deepfake video of a politician, and a synthetic voice waveform.
- **Color Palette:** Dark, ominous, glitch effects.
- **On-Screen Text:** "Can you trust your eyes and ears?"
- **Audio:** Unsettling, low-frequency hum building up to a crescendo.

## Scene 2: The Solution (0:10 - 0:20)
- **Visual Setup:** The screen clears. The FiduScan shield logo appears in a burst of bright indigo and emerald light, transitioning into the clean, modern glassmorphism UI of the landing page.
- **Color Palette:** Indigo, white, deep black.
- **On-Screen Text:** "FiduScan: AI Forensic Detection"
- **Audio:** A clean, satisfying "whoosh" or chime. Upbeat, professional background track begins.

## Scene 3: User Login (0:20 - 0:25)
- **Visual Setup:** Close-up of the cursor moving to the "Sign In" button. A smooth, frosted-glass modal appears. A dummy email is entered. The screen smoothly transitions to the Scanner Dashboard.
- **Focus:** The speed and elegance of the UI transition.

## Scene 4: Image Detection Demo (0:25 - 0:35)
- **Visual Setup:** The user drags a generated portrait (e.g., this-person-does-not-exist) into the drop zone.
- **Action:** The skeleton loader pulses. Within a second, the Result Card drops in. 
- **Focus:** Zoom in on the "AI GENERATED" pill, the 98% confidence score, and the toggle switching to the Grad-CAM Heatmap overlay over the face.

## Scene 5: Audio & Video Detection (0:35 - 0:45)
- **Visual Setup:** Split-screen layout.
- **Left Action (Audio):** An audio waveform is uploaded. The UI shows the transition from raw audio to a Log-Mel Spectrogram, and the result outputs "SYNTHETIC VOICE".
- **Right Action (Video):** A video is uploaded. The UI breaks it down into "Temporal", "Frames", and "Audio" scores, aggregating into a final verdict.
- **Focus:** The versatility and multimodal nature of the platform.

## Scene 6: Scan History (0:45 - 0:50)
- **Visual Setup:** The cursor clicks the "History" tab. A clean table populated with recent scans (images, audio, video) slides into view.
- **Action:** Cursor hovers over a previous scan, highlighting the persistent data state.

## Scene 7: Billing & Usage (0:50 - 0:55)
- **Visual Setup:** The user navigates to the "Settings" > "Usage" dashboard.
- **Focus:** Close-up on the percentage utilization rings. A ring is shown at 85% with an amber "Approaching Limit" pulse.
- **Action:** Cursor clicks "Upgrade Limits", showing the Stripe checkout portal integration.

## Scene 8: Developer Portal (0:55 - 1:05)
- **Visual Setup:** Switch to the Developer Portal tab. 
- **Action:** The user clicks "Generate Key". The UI masks the secret key instantly, demonstrating enterprise-grade security. A simulated terminal snippet shows a `curl` request succeeding.
- **On-Screen Text:** "Enterprise-grade API."

## Scene 9: Call to Action (1:05 - 1:15)
- **Visual Setup:** Zoom out back to the full dashboard, which fades into the FiduScan logo on a clean, dark background.
- **On-Screen Text:** "Start Scanning Free."
- **Audio:** The background track concludes on a strong, resolving chord.
